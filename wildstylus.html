WILDSTYLUS
DOWNTOWN LOS ANGELES, CALIFORNIA : MSAT '22
A civic campus that explores the relation of architecture to landscape in a harsh Southern California desert climate.While evoking the natural ecology of the existing site, the composition provides room for a resilient new ecology to evolve.
Additionally, the project utilizes layers of structures to provide a balance of solar shading and exposure to natural daylighting to underground spaces .
